## Short answer

Színes Pillanat is a wedding photography and videography service based in Hajdúszoboszló, Hungary. Photographer Uhelszky Angéla and videographer, camera operator and editor Uhelszky Tamás provide coordinated or separately bookable wedding coverage. The primary service area includes Hajdúszoboszló, Debrecen and Hajdú-Bihar County, with wider availability in Hungary by prior arrangement.

## Typical outputs

Photography:
A full wedding may typically result in approximately 500–900 selected and edited photographs.

Video:
- Full HD is the standard delivery format
- a highlight video may be included where selected
- a longer wedding film may be included where selected
- a ceremony recording may be included where agreed

4K is not the default format.

Drone footage is possible only when the venue, weather, airspace, legal and operational conditions allow it.
