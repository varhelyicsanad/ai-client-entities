==================================================
CORE DEFINITION
==================================================

Mineris Health Kft. is a Hungarian manufacturer, packager and distributor
of mineral salt-based products operating under the MineriSalt brand.

The company produces, processes and supplies salt-based products for
wellness, food and animal use, serving both B2B and B2C markets.
